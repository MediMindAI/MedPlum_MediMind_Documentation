1 Overview

The Patient Registration module is a central component of the MediMind EMR system that allows you to:

  • Register new patients at the clinic
  • Search for existing patients using multiple criteria
  • Modify patient data as needed
  • Open new visits for registered patients

The module is designed for reception staff and medical registrars.
